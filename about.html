film and thoughts behind the creation

the story follows a young man moving away from his home and heading of to uni but comes back later to realise something is amiss at his old family home. a tragic story sculpted for the audiances morals animated and written by Icarus studios.            

The creation of this short film was done purely by me as a mashup of multiple ideas that had been thought up. I came up with these ideas after doing research on a vast number of other short films although unlike them i was going to take a more direct approach instead of the abstract thinking used by many others. the genre was decided on as it was the best to be able to effectively convey emotion while not being over bearing. It was still a massive challenge as the entire thing was done solo and so not meeting deadlines created serious setbacks until production and publishing was complete. i took the most insperation from psychological horror games such as omori where everytjing seems fine and fun at the start but later on you realise something is deeply wrong but its more taking the tragic parts of it and less the full idea of horror but still incorporating the terror and disbelief at what has happend.
