Press kit - Icarus Studios

Short Film Solus

Icarus Studios is an independant production studio based in Harrogate, UK working on the production of a short animated film called Solus. We started in 27/10/2025 and have been working on our short film solus since we began the studio. The film was created based on the 50/50 split between two ideas that were presented to a group of people,  so instead of just using one and causing there to only be half the possible audience with the final product, we decided how about we combine them together and came up with our first ever animation Solus.
